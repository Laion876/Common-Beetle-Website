common beetle

The history and characteristics of the common beetle

Lore

The Common Beetle is a creature that inhabits fields and forests, known for its resilience and determination.

features

  • Average size: 1.5 cm
  • Color: Dark brown with bright highlights
  • habitat: Fields, forests and gardens
  • Behavior: Active during the day and night

Special ability

  • last breath:Attack speed increases dramatically as his life energy depletes. The closer he gets to death, the faster he becomes.
